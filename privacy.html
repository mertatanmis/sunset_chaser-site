Privacy Policy

Sunset Chaser respects your privacy and is transparent about how we handle your data.

Information we collect

  • Account details: email address and unique user ID for authentication and account management.
  • Profile details: display name and avatar you choose to share.
  • Location data: current location (if you enable permission), saved locations, and reminder schedules for sunset alerts.
  • Community content: sunset photos, optional notes, and location tags you upload.
  • Notification token: used to send sunset reminder notifications.
  • Support communications: information you send to us if you contact support.

How we use your information

  • Provide sunset times, forecasts, and quality scores for your locations.
  • Send reminders when you schedule sunset alerts.
  • Display community posts and profile information to other users.
  • Improve app stability and respond to support requests.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal data. We share limited data with trusted providers to run the app:

  • Supabase: authentication, database, and storage.
  • OpenWeather: weather data for forecasts.
  • Google and Apple Sign-In: optional OAuth login providers.
  • Expo Notifications: delivery of scheduled alerts.

Public content

Community photos, profile names, and avatars are visible to other Sunset Chaser users. Please only share content you are comfortable making public.

Your choices

  • You can manage location permissions in your device settings.
  • You can disable notifications at any time.
  • You can update or delete your community posts and profile.
  • You can delete your account in the app or by contacting support.

Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your profile and related data are removed from our systems.
