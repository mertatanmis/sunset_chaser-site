How it works

Add a location, see the forecast, set reminders, and share your sunset moments.

  • Search for a location or use your current location (optional permission).
  • Check the sunset time, weather quality, and cloud cover.
  • Set a reminder so the app notifies you before sunset.
  • Upload a photo and tag the location to share with the community.
Sunset Chaser is designed to be transparent about data use. Review the Privacy Policy below for full details on what is collected and why.

Data & privacy at a glance

We collect only what is needed to deliver the core features of the app.

  • Email + account ID: to create and secure your account.
  • Location data: to provide local sunset times, forecasts, and reminders (optional and user-controlled).
  • Community photos: to share your sunset posts with other users.
  • Profile details: display name and avatar for community profiles.
  • Notification token: to deliver sunset reminder alerts.

Transparency promise

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • You can manage permissions in your device settings.
  • You can delete your account and content at any time.
  • Community posts are public to other users of the app.
